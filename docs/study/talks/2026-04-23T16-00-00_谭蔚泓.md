## 讲座简介
Modern medicine has fully entered the era of molecular medicine, which requires in-depth understanding, diagnosis, treatment and prevention of major diseases at the molecular level. A comprehensive analysis of various functional molecules in living organisms is the key to achieving molecular classification of diseases and "face-recognition-like" multi-parameter precise diagnosis and treatment. However, despite tremendous advances in high-throughput nucleic acid sequencing technologies, the high-throughput, high-precision and quantitative characterization of functional molecules such as proteins, metabolites and carbohydrates at the single-cell level remains an urgent challenge. As functional nucleic acid molecules capable of specifically binding to targets, aptamers not only possess precise recognition capability, but also enable high-throughput screening, high-throughput sequencing and high-throughput synthesis. Based on these unique advantages, we propose the innovative concept of "Aptomics = aptamer + Omics". It aims to convert the powerful capacity of high-throughput sequencing into systematic understanding of functional molecules in living organisms that cannot be directly sequenced, so as to drive the transformation of multi-dimensional molecular diagnosis and treatment and promote breakthrough advances in medicine and biology.
