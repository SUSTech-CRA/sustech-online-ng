## 主讲人简介
杜娟教授（Juan Du）现任美国西北大学教授。她的研究聚焦于感官感知的分子机制，特别是人体如何通过神经元离子通道检测温度、味道等外界刺激，并将这些信号传递至大脑以诱发相应反应。杜娟教授拥有深厚的生物化学与结构生物学背景，在德国弗莱堡大学获得化学博士学位，并在美国Vollum Institute完成博士后研究。她领导的实验室致力于破解感觉神经科学领域的未解之谜，重点关注温度感知与味觉感知的生物物理机制。

## 讲座简介
Temperature profoundly affects macromolecular function, particularly in proteins with temperature sensitivity. However, its impact is often overlooked in biophysical studies typically performed at non-physiological temperatures, potentially leading to inaccurate mechanistic and pharmacological insights. This is demonstrated in our present study of TRPM4, a temperature-sensitive Ca2+-activated ion channel. By studying TRPM4 prepared at physiological temperature using single-particle cryo-electron microscopy, we discovered a “warm” conformation, distinct from those observed at lower temperatures...
