## 讲座简介
This talk focuses on empowering LLMs with greater agency. We first demonstrate the impact of liberating models from the prisons of human-engineered context. By enabling models to actively manage their own state, we achieved a 40% absolute accuracy gain on the BrowseComp-Plus task. We then introduce a paradigm for native meta-evolution, where agents spontaneously explore unknown environments and adapt via self-generated world knowledge, independent of human-defined rewards or workflows. Ultimately, we show how this self-generated knowledge enables a lightweight 30B Qwen3 model to outperform the unassisted Gemini-2.5-Pro and Kimi K2.5, establishing a new paradigm for truly autonomous, evolving agents.
